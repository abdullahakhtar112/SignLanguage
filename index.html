What is Signify?



We are a group of individuals that want to make a difference for the visually impaired by providing them with an application that can take away the communication barrier with a computer. We believe that in this world of technology, people with natural disabilities can and need to be able to interact with machines just as we do. We believe that technology has become an essential element of our existence. We have applied the latest AI and Deep Learning methodologies to our application and we have trained our models extensively over thousands of sample data sets. Although this is currently a prototype , we believe that we can grow our system to serve as a large scale, commercial application that will be deployed on Android and iOS.
